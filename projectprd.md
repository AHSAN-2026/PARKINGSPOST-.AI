# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Name

Parking Spot Finder

## Product Vision

To help drivers find, reserve, and navigate to available parking spots in real-time, reducing time spent searching for parking and improving urban mobility.

---

# Problem Statement

Drivers waste significant time searching for parking spaces, causing:

* Traffic congestion
* Fuel wastage
* Driver frustration
* Increased pollution

The app will provide real-time parking availability and navigation assistance.
