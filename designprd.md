# ParkingSpot AI - CURA Inspired Design System

## Project Overview
ParkingSpot AI is a smart parking discovery platform that helps drivers find available parking spaces in real-time.

The design inspiration comes from CURA Climate's modern industrial aesthetic:
- Minimalist layouts
- Bold typography
- Large whitespace
- Grid-based structure
- High contrast visuals
- Professional climate-tech feel

---

## Design Theme

### Theme Name
Industrial Minimalism

### Design Philosophy
Clean interfaces with strong typography and spacious layouts that emphasize trust, technology, and usability.
